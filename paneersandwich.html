PANEER SANDWICH

INGREDIENTS

INGREDIENTS FOR PANEER BREAD SANDWICH

  • 8 Slice Bread
  • 1/2 Cup Butter/Ghee/Oil

INGREDIENTS FOR PANEER STUFFING FOR SANDWICH

  • 200 gram paneer
  • 2 tablespoon Mustard Oil
  • 1 inch Ginger Piece
  • 6 cloves Garlic
  • 2 Onions
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • 1 Capsicum
  • 1/2 teaspoon Cumin Seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon Mustard Seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon Fenugreek Seeds
  • 2 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon fennel seeds powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon chilli flakes
  • 1/2 teaspoon amchur powder
  • 2 teaspoon tomato ketchup
  • 1 teaspoon kasuri methi (crushed)
  • 1 teaspoon salt (or as per your taste)
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1 tablespoon fresh coriander (finely chopped)

HOW TO MAKE PANEER STUFFING FOR SANDWICH

  • Firstly, chop the vegetables: ginger, garlic, onions, tomatoes, capsicum, anc cut the paneer into cubes
  • Heat 2 tablespoon oil in a kadai and add cumin, mustard and fenugreek seeds and saute them for a few seconds
  • Additionally, add finely chopped garlic and saute for a minute or till the garlic is light brown.
  • Further, add chopped onions and saute for about 2 minutes on low medium heat till the onions are translucent. Keep stirring often.
  • Now, add chopped tomatoes, finely chopped ginger, and salt. Mix well and cover the kadai/pan with a lid
  • Cook them for about 2 minutes on a medium-low flame till they soften and become pulpy. Keep stirring in between.
  • Further add turmeric powder, red chilli powder, coriander powder, fennel seeds powder and mix well.
  • Cover the kadai/pan with a lid and cook them for about a minute.
  • Give all the ingredients a stir.
  • Add chooped capsicum and 1 tablespoon of water and mix well.
  • Cover the kadai and cook them for 2 minutes on low-medium heat and keep stirring in between.
  • Further, add paneer, salt, garam masala, amchur powder, chili flakes and mix all ingredients gently.
  • Cook them for a minute.
  • Now add tomato ketchup, kasuri methi, mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes on low-medium heat or until the flavours are well absorbed. Keep stirring gently in between.
  • Add chopped fresh coriander and mix well.
  • After that, mash the paneer with a spatula and mix it well.
  • Finally, paneer stuffing for sandwich is ready now.

HOW TO MAKE PANEER SANDWICH

  • Firstly, take the two slices of bread.
  • Now spread 2 tablespoon of prepared paneer sabzi.
  • Further, cover the paneer stuffing with another bread slice.
  • Press gently the paneer bread sandwich.
  • Heat up a grill pan or tawa on medium heat and grease some butter/ghee/oil on it.
  • Alternatively, you can toast this on a sandwich maker or griller too.
  • After that, place the sandwich on a hot tawa.
  • Press down gently with a spatula and cook for a few minutes till it turns golden brown in color.
  • Now spread the butter/ghee on the other side.
  • Flip over the sandwich and cook from another side on low flame.
  • Further, press down gently with a spatula and grill the sandwich until both sides are crispy and golden brown in color.
  • Finally, cut half the sandwich and serve these crispy and delicious paneer sandwiches with sauce and chutney.
