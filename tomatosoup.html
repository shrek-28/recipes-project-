TOMATO SOUP

The Tomato soup has a smooth and creamy consistency with a light tanginess from the tomatoes. The soup is easy to make and doesn't take much time, and hence is perfect for lazy days.

RECIPE

  • Firstly chop 500 to 520 grams of tomatoes (5 medium sized tomatoes), 45 grams onion (1 medium onion) and 4 medium sized garlic cloves.
    • Cup measurements would be 4 cups chopped tomatoes, ⅓ cup chopped onions and 1 teaspoon chopped garlic.
  • Take 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a medium to large sauce pan or pan. Keep the heat to a low or medium-low.
    • Add 1 medium sized tej patta (Indian bay leaf). Fry for 2 to 3 seconds. If you do not have tej patta, then skip adding it.
  • Then add 4 medium-sized chopped garlic cloves.
  • Sauté garlic for some seconds or till their raw aroma goes away. No need to brown the garlic.
  • Add the chopped onions.
  • Stir and mix well.
  • Stirring often sauté onions till translucent.
  • Now add 2 tablespoons whole wheat flour (atta). You can also use all purpose flour (maida).
  • Mix well and begin to sauté the flour on a low heat stirring often.
  • Sauté for about 1 to 2 minutes or till the raw aroma of flour goes away.
  • Then add the chopped tomatoes.
  • Mix them very well.
  • Now add 2 cups water. Instead of water, you can also use veg stock.
  • Add 10 large cashews.
  • Then add ½ teaspoon crushed black pepper or add as per taste.
  • Add salt as per taste.
  • Stir and mix well
  • Cover the pan with a lid and simmer the soup mixture on a medium-low heat.
  • In between do check when the mixture is simmering.
  • Simmer till the tomatoes have softened. The tomatoes have to lose their rawness and should soften well. Then switch off the heat and let the soup mixture become warm.
  • Then pour the soup mixture in a blender jar. Remove the tej patta (Indian bay leaf) from the soup mixture before adding the soup mixture to the jar. Do use a good blender.
  • Blend to a smooth consistency. There should be no chunks of onions, tomatoes or cashews. You can also use an electric immersion blender and blend everything directly in the pan itself.
  • Now pour the ground soup mixture in the same pan.
  • Keep the pan on stove top on a low to medium-low heat and gently heat the soup.
  • When the homemade cream of tomato soup becomes hot, switch off the heat and add 2 to 3 basil leaves (chiffonade or chopped).
    • You can also add any herb of your choice. Instead of basil leaves, you can use cilantro leaves, parsley, oregano, sage, rosemary or any herb you like.
  • Serve cream of tomato soup hot. While serving you can garnish with a few chopped basil leaves or with some sprinkling of crushed black pepper. Serve it with some toasted bread or baguette.
