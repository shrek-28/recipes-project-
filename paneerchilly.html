PANEER CHILLY

Paneer Chilly is one of the popular vegetarian appetizers served in the Indian Chinese restaurants. Crisp batter fried paneer is tossed in slightly sweet, spicy, hot and tangy chilly sauce
While Garlic and green chillies bring in hot and pungent aroma, various sauces like chilli sauce, soya sauce and vinegar are used to impart some Chinese flavors. Veggies like onions, spring onions and bell pepper add body to the dish and corn starch brings an essential silky and smooth feel to the chilli paneer sauce.

FOR BATTER

  • 2 tablespoon refined flour
  • 2 tablespoon corn starch
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon corn starch (for Coating Paneer)
  • 250 gram paneer, cut in cubes
  • Oil to deep fry

FOR CHILLI PANEER SAUCE

  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon garlic, finely chopped
  • 2 dry red chilli, roughly chopped
  • 1 tablespoon celery, chopped
  • 1 medium onion, cut in quarters
  • 1 small capsicum, cut in cubes
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 fresh green and red chilli, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon green chilli sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sweet and sour sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon corn flour slurry (corn flour + water mixed)
  • Handful of spring onion, sliced (white with Green part)

GARNISH

  • Spring Onions, Chopped
  • Toasted Sesame Seeds

RECIPE

  • For coating paneer, add refined flour, corn flour, salt and water to a bowl, mix well and set aside till in use.
  • Add the corn starch to the paneer cubes and coat well, pour over the prepared batter and mix well coating all the paneer cubes evenly.
  • Deep fry the coated paneer cubes till light golden, drain on absorbent paper. Set aside till in use.
  • Heat oil in a pan, add ginger, garlic, whole red chilli and celery. Mix well and sauté for a few minutes.
  • Add onions and capsicum, sauté till onions are fragrant.
  • Add soy sauce and toss well to coat. Add fresh red and green chillies, sauté for a few seconds.
  • Add green chilli sauce, sweet & sour sauce, mix and toss well.
  • Add little water, sugar and cook till it melts.
  • Add some more water and the corn flour slurry, cook till the sauce thickens.
  • Add the fried paneer cubes and spring onions, toss well.
  • Garnish with spring onions and toasted sesame seeds.
  • Serve immediately.
